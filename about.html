About Razka Global Trade

Razka Global Trade is an Indonesian export-oriented company specializing in premium coconut-based products, with a strong focus on high-quality coconut briquettes for shisha and BBQ. We collaborate with local farmers and certified manufacturers to ensure consistent quality and international standards.

Our mission is simple: empowering local producers while connecting Indonesia’s rich natural resources to the global market. We prioritize strict quality control, sustainable sourcing, and transparent communication to guarantee reliable service for all international buyers.

With a commitment to professionalism and global competitiveness, Razka Global Trade proudly brings the best of Indonesia’s coconut products to the world.
